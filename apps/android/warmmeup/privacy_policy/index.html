Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 28, 2025

Introduction

WarmMeUp ("we", "our", or "the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect personally identifiable information. The only data accessed is device sensor data (e.g., battery temperature) to provide the app's functionality.

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected device information solely to power the application's heating visualization features and to improve app performance. No data is shared with third parties.

Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Vibration: To trigger device vibration when the app is running.
  • Battery Access: To read battery temperature and adjust visual effects accordingly.

Feedback

If you choose to provide feedback via the app, your comments and email will only be used to respond to your inquiry. We do not store or share this information for any other purpose.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics or advertising services.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the information accessed by the app. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this page with an updated effective date.
