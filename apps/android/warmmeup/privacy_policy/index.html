Privacy Policy – WarmMeUp App

Effective Date: December 22, 2025

WarmMeUp (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

WarmMeUp is a simple handwarmer app. We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email, location, or device identifiers.

The app may temporarily access:

  • Device battery and temperature readings – used only to display internal sensor values on the screen.
  • Vibration feature – to provide the handwarmer experience.

No data is sent to us or any third party. All information stays on your device.

2. Feedback

If you choose to send feedback using the “Send Feedback” button in the app, your email content will be sent directly to our email address.

  • Feedback may include your written text, app version, and optionally your device theme or intensity settings.
  • This information is stored only in your email and is not collected automatically by the app.

3. Cookies and Tracking

WarmMeUp does not use cookies, trackers, or analytics services. We do not track your activity outside of the app.

4. Third-Party Services

WarmMeUp does not integrate third-party services that collect personal information.

5. Data Security

Because we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information stored on servers. The app may temporarily store theme preferences and last intensity settings on your device using Android’s local storage (DataStore). This is used only to restore your preferred settings.

6. Children’s Privacy

WarmMeUp is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The updated version will be included in the app or on our website.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: youremail@example.com
