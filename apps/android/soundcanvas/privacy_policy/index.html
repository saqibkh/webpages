Privacy Policy

SoundCanvas is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the SoundCanvas Android application.

By using this app, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use

SoundCanvas does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • No user accounts or sign-in required
  • No analytics or tracking services
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No personal identifiers collected

All sounds are played locally on your device.

Permissions

SoundCanvas may request the following permissions:

  • Audio Playback – Required to play sounds
  • Foreground Service – Used to allow continuous background playback with a persistent notification

These permissions are used solely to provide the app’s core functionality.

Background Playback & Notifications

SoundCanvas supports background audio playback. When audio is playing, a notification may be shown to allow playback control.

No data is collected or transmitted through notifications.

Third-Party Services

SoundCanvas does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children’s Privacy

SoundCanvas does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

Since no personal data is collected at all, the app is safe for general audiences.

Data Security

Because SoundCanvas does not collect or store personal data, there is no risk of personal data exposure through the app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
